Tool Directory



Account Finder

A tool to find online accounts based on a known username or email address.

Enumerating potential user accounts.

DNS Lookup

Search for publicly available DNS records based on a known URL.

Find subdomains, mail servers, and other domain assets.

Port Scanner

Scan server ports.

Find other running services that may be vulnerable or discover hidden HTTP services.

Subdomain Fuzzer

Fuzz for hidden subdomains.

Find new functionality to grow attack surface.

Vhost Fuzzer

Fuzz for hidden Vhosts.

Grow attack surface that is not available in DNS records.

Directory Fuzzer

Fuzz for hidden directories.

Grow attack surface and find hidden endpoints or pages.
